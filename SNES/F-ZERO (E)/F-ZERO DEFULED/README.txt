F-ZERO DEFULED Version 1.0
--------------------------

Do you think that F-ZERO is not challenging enough?
With this ROM hack, F-ZERO plays exactly like you are used too, but without the ability to recharge your power.
Good luck with Master Mode! ;)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This hack is meant to be applied to a F-ZERO (E) ROM for the SNES!

You can use DeltaPatcher to apply this hack to your ROM.
DeltaPatcher can be downloaded here: https://github.com/marco-calautti/DeltaPatcher
